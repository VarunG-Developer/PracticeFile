SUPER MARIO

Nitendo Entertainment

Super Mario, created by Nintendo, is one of the most iconic and influential video game franchises of all time. The series debuted in 1985 with "Super Mario Bros." for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES). This game introduced players to Mario, a plumber who embarks on a quest to rescue Princess Peach from the evil Bowser. Its innovative side-scrolling gameplay, memorable characters, and catchy music revolutionized the gaming industry.
Mario first appeared in the 1981 arcade game "Donkey Kong," where he was initially known as "Jumpman." His distinctive red hat, blue overalls, and mustache made him a recognizable character. "Super Mario Bros." built upon this foundation, adding intricate level designs, power-ups like the Super Mushroom and Fire Flower, and a two-player mode featuring Mario's brother, Luigi.
